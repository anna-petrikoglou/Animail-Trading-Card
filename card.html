The Arctic Wolf

"Also known as Melville Island wolf, relatively unafraid of people. Can be coaxed to approach them in some areas."

Name: White Wolf
Habitat: Canadian Arctic Archipelago
Behaviour: Occasionally aggresive
Condition: Endangered

Medium-sized subspecies of Canis lupus, distinguished from the northwestern wolf by its smaller size, its whiter coloration, its narrower braincase, and larger carnassials. Since 1930, there has been a progressive reduction in size in Arctic wolf skulls, which is likely the result of wolf-dog hybridization.
